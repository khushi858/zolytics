Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Zolytics training program?

The Zolytics training program aims to train and certify users in using Zolytics. With sessions covering everything from basic use to advanced techniques, this training program is the ideal opportunity to learn new concepts and gain knowledge to help you build solutions for your business.

What are the programs offered online?

We offer Stage 1 training for Business Users and Stage 2 training for Developers, plus free webinar series covering specialized topics.

What certifications are available?

Upon completion, participants receive official Zolytics certification recognized across the industry.

Why should I attend?

Gain hands-on experience, learn best practices, get direct access to experts, and earn valuable certification for your career.
