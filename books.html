Inventory Management – Problems & Solutions

Common inventory challenges and how Zoho Books solves them

Problems Businesses Face
  • Stock mismatch between physical and system records
  • No real-time visibility across warehouses
  • Overstocking or frequent stock-outs
  • Manual stock tracking using Excel
How Zoho Books Solves This
  • Real-time stock tracking with automatic updates
  • Multi-warehouse location tracking

  • Batch and serial number tracking
