Complete financial management suite

Everything you need to manage your business finances efficiently and professionally.

Smart Invoicing & Billing

Create professional invoices with customizable templates, automate recurring bills, send payment reminders, and accept online payments through multiple gateways.

Expense Tracking & Claims

Record expenses on-the-go, scan receipts automatically, track mileage, categorize spending, and submit employee expense claims with approval workflows.

Banking & Reconciliation

Connect your bank accounts, auto-import transactions, match them with invoices and bills, and reconcile your accounts in minutes instead of hours.

Inventory Management

Track stock levels across warehouses, manage purchase orders, set reorder points, track serial and batch numbers, and get low stock alerts automatically.

Financial Reports & Analytics

Generate profit & loss statements, balance sheets, cash flow reports, tax summaries, and custom reports. Get real-time insights with interactive dashboards.

GST & Tax Compliance

GST-compliant invoicing, automatic tax calculations, e-invoicing, GSTR-1, GSTR-3B filing support, TDS tracking, and comprehensive tax reports for easy compliance.

Project Time Tracking

Track billable and non-billable hours, manage project budgets, create invoices from timesheets, and monitor project profitability in real-time.

Vendor & Purchase Management

Manage vendor profiles, track purchase orders and bills, schedule payment runs, maintain payment history, and get vendor performance insights.

Client & Customer Portal

Give customers a self-service portal to view invoices, make payments, download statements, track project progress, and communicate directly with you.
