Trial Balance Match

We import Trial Balance and Journal reports from QuickBooks and reconcile them against Zoho Books account-wise. Net difference must be zero before go-live.

Mismatch Resolution Framework

Any mismatch is resolved using controlled methods: manual journals, transaction recreation, or opening balance adjustment â€” never blind overrides.

Audit Trail Preservation

All adjustments retain original dates, references, currencies, and notes to ensure traceability during audits or statutory reviews.

Multi-Currency Balance Validation

Foreign currency balances are verified using correct exchange rates, revaluation entries, and base-currency parity checks to avoid FX discrepancies.

Opening Balance Lockdown

Once verified and approved, opening balances are locked to prevent accidental edits, ensuring long-term financial data integrity.

Pre Go-Live Sign-Off

A final reconciliation report is shared for client sign-off, confirming balances, adjustments, and compliance before system go-live.
