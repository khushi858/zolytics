What is Zoho CRM?

Zoho CRM is a comprehensive cloud-based customer relationship management platform designed to help businesses of all sizes manage their sales, marketing, and customer support operations efficiently. It provides a unified system to track customer interactions, automate workflows, and gain actionable insights into your business performance.

With Zoho CRM, you can centralize all customer data, streamline communication across teams, and create personalized experiences that drive customer satisfaction and loyalty.
