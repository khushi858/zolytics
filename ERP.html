Zoho ERP Modules We Implement

Comprehensive suite covering all aspects of your business operations

Zoho Books - Financial Management

Complete accounting solution with GST compliance, multi-currency support, automated invoicing, expense tracking, bank reconciliation, and advanced financial reporting aligned with IFRS and ASC standards.

Zoho Inventory - Supply Chain Management

Real-time inventory tracking across multiple warehouses, automated reorder points, batch and serial number tracking, barcode scanning, and seamless integration with sales and purchasing.

Zoho Commerce - Omnichannel Retail

Unified commerce platform for online stores, physical retail, and marketplaces. Integrated POS, payment processing, order management, and customer engagement tools.

Zoho People - HR & Payroll

Complete HR management including employee records, attendance tracking, leave management, performance reviews, and automated payroll with EPF, ESI, TDS, and compliance.

Zoho Billing - Subscription Management

Automate the entire subscription lifecycle from quote to revenue recognition. Recurring billing, proration, dunning management, and seamless integration with finance.

Zoho Analytics - Business Intelligence

Advanced analytics and reporting with AI-powered insights from Zia. Custom dashboards, predictive analytics, and real-time visibility across all business functions.

Zoho Creator - Custom Applications

Low-code platform to build custom ERP modules, workflows, and automations tailored to your unique business processes without extensive coding.

Zoho Expense - Spend Management

Automated expense reporting, policy enforcement, multi-level approvals, corporate card reconciliation, and integration with accounting for complete spend visibility.
